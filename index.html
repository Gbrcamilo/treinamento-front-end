Hulk

Bioscience genius Dr. Bruce Banner focused his major studies on the effects of gamma radiation on humans, working alongside Dr. Betty Ross at a Virginia University lab...

Having destroyed his lab as the creature, and inadvertently harming Ross in the process, Banner slipped away to avoid capture and study by the Army...

Spider-Man

A bite from a spider somehow granted teenager Peter Parker its arachnid abilities and instead of using them for personal gain, he decided to help others with them...

Calling himself Spider-Man and sporting a pair of web-shooting devices he’d constructed, Parker wound up in internet videos which attracted the attention of Tony Stark...

Iron Man

Genius inventor and billionaire Tony Stark created the Iron Man suit to save his own life and escape captivity. After returning home, he continued to refine his armor to protect the world as Iron Man...

His tech innovations and heroism have made him a powerful force, though his bold personality sometimes brings its own challenges...

Thor

Born 1500 years ago to Odin and Frigga, the king and queen of fabled Asgard, Thor grew into a mighty warrior and protector of his home and people...

Alone and unable to return to Asgard, Thor met scientists Jane Foster and Erik Selvig in the midst of their investigation into strange activity...

Hawkeye

Clint Barton, also known as Hawkeye, is a master archer and former special agent with S.H.I.E.L.D. Known for his sharp eye and skill with a bow, he’s a valuable ally in any fight...

Despite having no superpowers, his dedication and accuracy make him one of the most formidable members of the Avengers...

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, known as Black Widow, is a former Russian spy and a master of combat. Recruited to the Avengers, she brings her skills and intelligence to the team...

Her past is filled with secrets and dark missions, but she’s committed to using her talents to help those in need...

Captain America

Steve Rogers, a frail young man who volunteered for an experiment that turned him into the super-soldier Captain America, now fights for justice and freedom...

With his shield and unwavering moral compass, he leads by example and remains a symbol of hope for all...
